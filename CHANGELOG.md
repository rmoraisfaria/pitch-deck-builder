### Added
- Initial release of Pitch Deck Builder
- Core four-phase workflow (Discovery, Planning, Execution, Refinement)
- Comprehensive SKILL.md with detailed guidance
- Three reference documents:
  - Pitch Deck Best Practices
  - Narrative Frameworks
  - Data Visualization Guide
- Support for multiple pitch deck types:
  - Investor pitches (pre-seed/seed, Series A+)
  - Sales and product pitches
  - Partnership pitches
  - Demo day decks
  - One-pagers
- Context-aware chunking strategy for large decks (>15 slides)
- HTML/CSS to PPTX workflow integration
- Narrative frameworks:
  - Hero's Journey
  - Problem-Agitate-Solve
  - Pixar Pitch
  - Before-After-Bridge
  - Epiphany Framework
- Comprehensive design principles
- Color strategy and accessibility guidelines
- Data visualization guidance for 8+ chart types
- Persuasion techniques and psychological principles
- Quality checklists for all major sections

## Version Details

### 1.0.0 - Initial Release

**Release Date**: 2024

This is the inaugural release of Pitch Deck Builder, providing a complete methodology for creating professional pitch decks from concept to delivery. The skill was developed based on:

- Extensive analysis of successful pitch decks across industries
- Investor feedback on what makes pitches compelling
- Presentation psychology and storytelling research
- Design best practices from presentation professionals
- Real-world pitch deck creation experience

The skill is production-ready and suitable for:
- First-time pitch deck creators
- Experienced presenters looking for systematic approach
- Sales professionals building customer presentations
- Founders raising capital
- Product teams launching new initiatives

All reference materials have been thoroughly tested and validated against current best practices.
